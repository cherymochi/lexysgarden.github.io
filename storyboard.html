Theme: Pastel Blossoms

I chose this theme as I have always been fond of Cherry Blossom trees and soft pastel colours. Looking at the trees when watching shows or playing games would always give me peace of mind. One day, I would like to visit Japan to see them in person. The colours pastel blue was chosen to emulate a sky and the accents of pink, to represent the blossoms that often fall from the cherry blossom trees.


Target Audience: General Public
