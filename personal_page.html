I took a Myers-Briggs test from a rather popular site to find out my MBTI and after completion, I received type INFJ-T. Reading through each of the traits listed under this type, i.e. the ones shown above, I was really able to relate to each of them in some manner or another. The trait that really stood out to me was Turbulent, as it had such a high percentage. In the description, persons with this trait are said to be incredibly self-concious and sensitive to stress and these can be seen as very negative traits, but reading further it states that those with a turbulent personality are success-driven and perfectionists. Personally, I find myself possessing more of the negative traits, however after consulting some of my friends there was a general consensus of me possessing the positive traits as well. They also agreed that the other traits listed in the image shown above are reminiscent of me. With this in mind, I agree that this test has some validity for my own personality.
As a small experiment, I asked some of my closer peers to take this test as well so I could evaluate their results and infer as to whether or not the Myers-Briggs test has validity in general. After assessing each of their results, I can safely assume the this personality test is indeed valid.
